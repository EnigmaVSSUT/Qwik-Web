UPCOMING EVENTS

Lift-Off-C

Date - 1st February 2021

Eagerly waiting to get started with programming, but don’t know how to start? Worry not, ‘cuz we got you covered.

CodeChef VSSUT Chapter, in collaboration with Enigma and DSC VSSUT, brings you the most awaited programme – LiftOff C which aims to help you get started with C programming.

Who can register? Anyone with a knack for learning. Whether you are totally new to programming or already have some knowledge about it, this is for you.

What will happen in this? We will guide you from the basics of programming to advanced topics so you can master in it. We will also conduct contests and fun quizzes with questions designed especially for you to try the skills you have learnt during the programme.

So, how will this work? Interested students will be divided into small groups and be assigned a mentor who will guide you, provide you with materials to study from, will conduct regular doubt clearing sessions and will always be there to help you whenever you get stuck. Sounds awesome, right?

What will you get from it? This will be a 4-6 weeks programme, by the end of which you can proudly say that you have mastered the skill of programming. Wait, there’s more. The students who will successfully complete the programme and participate in all the contests will be awarded with an official certificate which you will be able to show off to whoever you want. Exciting, right? Go ahead and fill out this form to register yourselves in this programme to LiftOff with C with us!

Registrations will close on 28th January 2021 and the program will start from 1st February 2021
