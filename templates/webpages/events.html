PAST EVENTS

Lift-Off-C

Date - 1st February 2021

CodeChef VSSUT Chapter, in collaboration with Enigma and DSC VSSUT, organized LiftOff C which aims to help students get started with C programming. Students were divided in 33 groups and were assigned mentors. Weekly classes were held. Students were provided with weekly curated assignments and doubt clearing classes. Online HackerEarth coding contest was organized to put thier gained knowledge to test.

ORIENTATION - CYBERNATED ESCAPADE

Date - 16th January 2021

The freshmen orientation for batch 2020-24 was successfully conducted by Team Enigma.The freshers were given a brief introduction about the club and working of various domains. They participated in huge numbers and were extremely inquisitive during the QnA session.

CODEIT

Online - 8th March 2020

Onsite - 13th March 2020

In our annual Techno-Management fest Samavesh 2020, Enigma conducted a coding contest CODE-IT, in which students from various institutes participated. The winners of the coding challenge were given cash prizes worth 15k!

GDG WORKSHOP

Date - 13th March 2020

In the annual Techno-Management fest Samavesh 2020 Enigma organised workshops on Machine learning using tensor flow, Blockchain and Google Cloud which were conducted by Google Developer Group, Bhubaneswar.

HACKTOBER FEST

Date - 2nd October 2020

Hacktoberfest is a month long celebration of open source software. Enigma in collaboration with DSC VSSUT organised a webinar on open source , Hacktoberfest and GSoC. Speakers Amogh Desai and Ayush Agarwal talked about their journeys of cracking GSoC and development in open source software.

HACKTOBER - GIT & GITHUB

Date - 11th October 2020

Enigma in collaboration with DSC VSSUT organised a meet up on the basics of Git and GitHub. Speaker Vartika Singh explained the viewers about how to access GitHub and work on its various operations like create and merge pull requests.
