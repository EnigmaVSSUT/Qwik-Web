About

Enigma-The Coding Club is the place to take that interest in technology and turn it into a productive skill. We aim to reach each and every student passionate about programming and guide them through their coding adventure. Enigma is for everyone regardless of their branch or current level of knowledge. We believe learning is more fun and efficient when we work a team. Enigma is for students who are curious problem solvers, responsible and team players. Our team consists of highly efficient members who regularly work on innovative projects from every aspect of advanced computing. For a programmer, challenges are a part of their lives daily routine.Enigma appreciates this habit by providing unlimited scope to participate in coding challenges held across the country. Since 2011,Enigma has over 200 students and mentors dedicated to establish coding culture in VSSUT.We also have a wide and supportive alumni base which are extremely well placed in tech giants around the globe and are ever present in the workings of the club.

What we do

These are the domains we work on.

Competitive Coding

Competitive programming can be defined as solving well defined problems but what counts here is the efficiency and accuracy of your code. With the fast rising demand for efficient coders, we are here to pave your way for competitive coding to provide you with better career opportunities and future goals.

Web Development

Web development refers to the building of website that cater to fulfilling various needs, adds value to an existing business. Get drawn to our wonderful UI &UX and think forward to start your journey in this domain.

App Development

Everybody today is aware of the ease that various apps provide today.Right from games, to classrooms, to shopping and many more. Get fascinated by the skillfully designed and user friendly apps, kick-start your journey in the field of app development with team-ENIGMA.

AI / ML

Machine learning allows the application of sophisticated algorithms that provide systems the ability to learn automatically and improve from previous experiences without being explicitly programmed. Are you curious to know how some sites predict your choices?The wait is over. Join us and avail the opportunity to explore and get exposed to a number of fascinating projects.

AR / VR

AR and VR bridges the gap between two worlds. HTC Vive, Sony PlayStation, Oculus Rift use VR for games like Pokemon Go where you might sit in the cockpit of a Starfighter. VR replaces your vision , AR adds more to it by overlaying information on top of it, as if you're wearing a pair of sunglasses. Want to experience the thrills of a different world and be able to design your own? Join us for a journey fill with excitement and adventures.

UX / UI Dsign

Great graphic design allows you to make a positive first impression on those looking on. It’s important to know that the graphic design that you choose to represent your brand can set you apart from your competition – whether negatively or positively. Ready to get your hands dirty with some beauty and creativity? Enigma will guide you to a wonderland of such imagery and help to create one!
